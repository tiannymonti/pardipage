Team Name
Renewable and Unconventional Energy Sources
Goals

To explore different forms of energy available in the environment to supply power to electronic devices that support the concept of nodesourcing. Information exchange and command-control sending are mainly considered through WiFi and / or cellular networks. The power-generation schemes configuration covers different voltage levels depending on the required solution. The fundamental pillar of the work of the group corresponds to maintaining a balance between resourcefulness and efficiency of the proposed solutions.

Methods and Technologies

Solar energy, wind energy, energy harvesters, power electronics, mixed-signal electronic design applications, web app, databases, Graphical User Interfaces, sensors, wireless communication systems, social networks.

Graphic Description

Research/Design Issues

Power-autonomous telemetry systems, weather information systems, off-grid and grid-tie photovoltaic systems, hybrid energy systems, low-power communication systems, low-cost transmission links, energy harvesters, scalable monitoring and control systems.
